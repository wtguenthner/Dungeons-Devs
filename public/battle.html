How To Play


Player will begin with the first turn
against the boss.

Player must choose from 1 of the 3
stat cards before deciding whether
to attack or defend.

Each card will boost the requisite
stat by the requisite amount for
ONE turn.

The difference between your attack
stat and the boss's defense stat will
be dealt to the boss's HP.

After your turn, the boss will either
attack you or defend against you.
